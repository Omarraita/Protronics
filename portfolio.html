Our Portfolio

Explore our innovative projects in electronics and engineering that showcase our expertise and commitment to excellence.

ECG Monitoring Patch

Developed a cutting-edge wearable ECG monitoring patch for continuous cardiac health assessment. The patch integrates a highly sensitive analog front-end circuit for precise signal acquisition, low-power wireless communication for data transmission, and advanced signal processing algorithms for real-time analysis. The project involved extensive hardware testing and optimization to meet stringent medical-grade certification standards, ensuring reliability, accuracy, and comfort for long-term use by patients.

Custom Wearable PCB Design

Engineered a compact and efficient PCB design for wearable devices, focusing on power efficiency, miniaturization, and robust performance. The project addressed the challenges of integrating multiple sensors, battery management systems, and communication modules into a small form factor. The PCB was rigorously tested under various environmental conditions to ensure durability and functionality in real-world scenarios. This design is now deployed in wearable fitness trackers and health monitoring devices, setting a new standard for innovation in the wearable electronics space.

Medtech Electronics Solution

Engineered hardware and software for a next-generation heart monitoring device, enhancing patient care and diagnostics. This solution incorporated custom-designed sensors and embedded firmware for real-time health data monitoring and analytics. The device achieved compliance with medical-grade safety and regulatory standards, making it a market leader in Medtech innovation.

Embedded Systems Development

Created a robust embedded system for a smart home application, integrating IoT functionality for seamless control. This system was designed with a focus on energy efficiency, real-time data communication, and user-friendly operation, providing a complete solution for modern smart living needs.
